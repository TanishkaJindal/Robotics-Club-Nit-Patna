THE ORIGIN

Founded in the heart of NIT Patna, the Robotics Club began as a small group of electronics enthusiasts in a dorm room. Today, we occupy the 3rd floor of the Incubation Center, armed with 3D printers, CNC machines, and a passion for automation.

Our philosophy is simple: Theory is optional. Execution is mandatory. We bridge the gap between textbook engineering and real-world mechanics.
